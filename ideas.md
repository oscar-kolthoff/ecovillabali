# Design Ideas for Eco Villa Sibang Kaja Website

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Tropical Modernism / Biophilic Minimalism
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Nature Integration**: Blur the lines between digital interface and organic imagery, mimicking the villa's open-air architecture.
  2. **Serene Luxury**: Convey high-end exclusivity through restraint, breathing room, and quiet elegance rather than opulence.
  3. **Sensory Digital Experience**: Evoke the "fragrance of cempaka" and "sound of the river" through visual textures and fluid motion.
  4. **Authentic Storytelling**: Focus on the narrative of the village (Sibang Kaja) and the lifestyle, not just the property specs.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: Warm off-whites (Rice Paper) and deep charcoal (Volcanic Stone) for high contrast but softer than black/white.
  - **Primary**: Deep Forest Green (representing the jungle) and Terracotta/Clay (representing traditional Balinese roof tiles and earth).
  - **Accent**: Muted Gold (Bamboo warmth) and Soft Sage (Rice terraces).
  - *Intent*: To ground the user in the earthiness of Bali while maintaining a sophisticated, clean palette suitable for a luxury sale.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Asymmetric & Overlapping**: Avoid rigid grids. Use overlapping image layers and text blocks to mimic the organic layering of a jungle.
  - **Split-Screen Scrolling**: One side fixed (visuals) while content scrolls, or vice versa, creating a guided tour feel.
  - **Full-Bleed Imagery**: Hero sections that take up the entire viewport to immerse the user immediately in the environment.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Organic Shapes**: Subtle border radii on images, but mixed with sharp architectural lines to reflect the villa's structure.
  - **Texture Overlays**: Very subtle grain or paper texture on background colors to remove the "digital flatness."
  - **Botanical Illustrations**: Line-art sketches of Cempaka flowers or bamboo leaves used as background watermarks or section dividers.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Slow & Smooth**: Scroll interactions should feel weighty and smooth (parallax), not jittery or fast.
  - **Hover Reveals**: Images that slightly zoom or reveal additional details/captions on hover, inviting exploration.
  - **Micro-interactions**: Buttons that "fill up" like water or grow organically when interacted with.

  **Animation**:
  - **Parallax Scrolling**: Background layers move slower than foreground content to create depth (jungle depth).
  - **Fade-Up & Reveal**: Text and images shouldn't just appear; they should gently rise or fade in as if emerging from the mist.
  - **Image Masks**: Images revealing themselves through organic wiping motions rather than simple opacity fades.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: *Playfair Display* or *Cinzel Decorative* (Serif) - Elegant, editorial, reflecting tradition and luxury.
  - **Body**: *DM Sans* or *Outfit* (Sans-serif) - Clean, modern, highly readable, providing a contemporary contrast to the headings.
  - **Hierarchy**: Large, dramatic headings with generous line height; small, tracked-out overlines for labels (e.g., "THE LOCATION").
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Neo-Brutalism x Balinese Craft
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Raw & Refined**: Contrast raw materials (concrete, stone textures in UI) with refined typography and layout.
  2. **Bold Geometry**: Use strong geometric shapes inspired by Balinese gates and temple structures.
  3. **High Impact**: Large typography and high-contrast imagery to make a bold statement about the property's uniqueness.
  4. **Directness**: Clear, unadorned presentation of facts and features, appealing to serious investors.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: Cool Concrete Grey and Stark White.
  - **Primary**: Electric Green (Vibrant jungle life) and Deep Indigo (Night sky).
  - **Accent**: Burnt Orange (Sunset/Offerings).
  - *Intent*: To position the villa as a modern architectural masterpiece, standing out from typical "resort" aesthetics.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Modular Grid**: Visible grid lines or distinct blocks separating content, reminiscent of architectural blueprints.
  - **Horizontal Scrolling**: Sections that scroll horizontally to showcase galleries or timelines, breaking the vertical monotony.
  - **Sticky Headers/Footers**: Prominent, always-visible navigation and CTA bars.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Thick Borders**: distinct borders around images and buttons.
  - **Monospaced Accents**: Use of monospaced fonts for technical details (sqm, price, coordinates).
  - **Architectural Lines**: decorative lines that connect elements, guiding the eye like a floor plan.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Snappy & Precise**: Interactions are instant and crisp. No soft fades; things snap into place.
  - **Cursor Effects**: Custom cursor that changes state (e.g., "View" or "Explore") when hovering over interactive zones.

  **Animation**:
  - **Slide-In**: Elements slide in from sides with momentum.
  - **Text Reveal**: Text characters appearing one by one or lines sliding up from masks.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: *Syne* or *Clash Display* - Unique, structural, artistic.
  - **Body**: *Space Grotesk* or *Archivo* - Technical, modern, geometric.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Ethereal Zen / Soft Minimalism
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Atmospheric**: Focus on the *feeling* of the space—light, air, sound.
  2. **Softness**: Rounded corners, blurred backdrops, soft shadows.
  3. **Flow**: The user journey should feel like a continuous, uninterrupted flow, like the Ayung River.
  4. **Intimacy**: Close-up photography, warm tones, personal narrative tone.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: Cream, Beige, Warm Grey.
  - **Primary**: Muted Olive, Dusty Pink (Sunset clouds), Soft Brown (Wood).
  - **Accent**: Champagne Gold.
  - *Intent*: To evoke a sense of calm, wellness, and retreat.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Centralized & Focused**: Single-column layouts with generous margins, focusing attention on one element at a time.
  - **Whitespace Dominance**: 50% of the screen is often whitespace (or "breath space").

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass effects for cards and overlays.
  - **Soft Gradients**: Backgrounds that shift subtly in color.
  - **Circular/Oval Frames**: Images framed in arches or circles to soften the edges.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Gentle**: Everything is soft. Buttons have soft shadows and glow on hover.
  - **Scroll-Linked**: Background colors change slowly as you scroll down the page (e.g., from Morning light to Evening mood).

  **Animation**:
  - **Float**: Elements gently float or bob.
  - **Blur-In**: Content blurs into focus.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: *Cormorant Garamond* or *Tenor Sans* - Delicate, expressive, human.
  - **Body**: *Lato* or *Mulish* - Friendly, round, approachable.
</idea>
